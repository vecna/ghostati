# Ghostati / Ghostmaxxing Naming Brief

## Summary

Ghòstati is the Italian-rooted name.
Ghostmaxxing is the proposed international-facing name.

The two names can coexist inside the same project universe:

- Ghòstati: local, Italian, NINA-rooted, culturally specific
- Ghostmaxxing: international, internet-native, easier to explain outside Italy
- Ghostyle: plugin/style unit, usable across both

## Why Ghostmaxxing works internationally

Ghostmaxxing has several advantages:

1. It is immediately legible to internet-native audiences.
2. It echoes an existing meme grammar without depending entirely on it.
3. It suggests “maximizing ghostliness”, which is funny and unexpected.
4. It keeps the ghost metaphor alive without needing Italian.
5. It can carry a lighter first impression while the content remains rigorous.

## Risk

The risk is that Ghostmaxxing could sound too unserious.

Counterbalance it with:
- precise technical copy
- strong references archive
- no false anonymity claims
- clear safety limitations
- investigative/reporting infrastructure

The meme opens the door; the evidence keeps people inside.

## Recommended public framing

Ghostmaxxing is the international name for Ghòstati: a browser-based lab for testing face-recognition camouflage, building Ghostyles, and documenting the history of anti-biometric appearance design.

## Short versions

Ghostmaxxing is a public lab for testing face-recognition camouflage.

Ghostmaxxing lets people build and test Ghostyles: browser-based visual interventions against face-recognition pipelines.

Ghostmaxxing is not about becoming invisible. It is about making face-recognition systems visible enough to test.

## What not to say

Avoid:

- Ghostmaxxing makes you anonymous.
- Maximize invisibility.
- Beat facial recognition.
- Become untrackable.
- The meme version of Ghòstati.
- A fun anti-surveillance filter.

## URL / information architecture options

### Option A: same project, language path

/ghostati/it/ -> Ghòstati
/ghostati/en/ -> Ghostmaxxing

Pros:
- clear language split
- one codebase
- one project identity
- easier SEO management

### Option B: same homepage, dual name

Ghòstati / Ghostmaxxing

Pros:
- immediate continuity
- no need to explain later
- useful during transition

Cons:
- title may feel crowded
- less clean for international launch

### Option C: Ghostmaxxing landing, Ghòstati as origin

International homepage:
Ghostmaxxing
“Originally developed as Ghòstati in the NINA context.”

Pros:
- clean international presentation
- preserves origin without overloading the headline

Cons:
- requires careful explanation to avoid looking like a separate project

## Recommendation

Use Option C for international launch.

Keep Ghòstati as the Italian/local identity and origin.
Use Ghostmaxxing as the English/international presentation.

## Relationship with Ghostyle

Ghostyle remains a strong term.

It works in both Italian and English:
- Ghostyle plugin
- Build a Ghostyle
- Ghostyle archive
- Ghostyle test

Ghostmaxxing can become the umbrella action.
Ghostyle remains the unit of intervention.

Example:

Ghostmaxxing happens through Ghostyles.

Or:

Build a Ghostyle. Test the read. Document the limits.
